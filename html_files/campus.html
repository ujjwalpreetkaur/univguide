Place Inside The Campus

ETERNAL UNIVERSITY

Eternal University is a private university located in Baru Sahib, Himachal Pradesh. It offers education in various fields like engineering, management, health sciences, and agriculture. The campus is known for its peaceful environment, modern facilities, and focus on both academics and values.

AKAL COLLEGE OF NURSING

Akal College of Nursing (ACN) – India’s first nursing college in HP, founded in 2008 under Eternal University. Offers B.Sc, M.Sc & Ph.D. programs. Accredited nationally, with strong clinical training and value‑based education for compassionate healthcare professionals.

APPLE A DAY

Apple A Day is a popular health food and juice corner inside the Eternal University campus. It offers fresh juices, fruits, healthy snacks, and light meals — a favorite spot for students and staff to grab a quick and nutritious bite.

NEW GIRLS HOSTEL

New Girls Hostel – A modern 9‑storey hostel at Eternal University, with ~756 fully furnished seats, shared rooms for friendly living, campus meals, and a safe, supportive environment.

AKAL ACADEMY

Akal Academy Baru Sahib – A residential school founded in 1986. Offers CBSE, IB PYP & Cambridge curricula in a spacious Himalayan campus. Known for value-based education, modern infrastructure, and a diverse student community.

IB

IB (PYP) – An international program for Nursery to Grade V that builds curiosity, confidence, and global understanding through activity-based learning.

GURUDWARA BARUSAHIB

Gurudwara Baru Sahib – A majestic spiritual center in the Valley of Divine Peace. Built from a humble mud hut in 1956, it now spans ~450 acres and anchors campus life with prayer, langar, and meditation.

KALGIDHAR CHARITABLE HOSPITAL

Kalgidhar Charitable Hospital – A 100+ bed charitable hospital on campus offering multi-specialty care, diagnostic services, emergency support, and free medical camps, reaching both university residents and rural communities.

SBI ATM

SBI ATM at Baru Sahib Open 24/7 near the New Girls Hostel. Accessible to all for cash withdrawals and balance checks.

BARUSAHIB POSTOFFICE

Barusahib Post Office (PIN 173101) – Campus branch for mail, parcels, savings, and official postal services. Typically open Mon–Sat, ~9 AM–5 PM.

GUEST ROOMS

Guest Rooms – A peaceful, fully furnished guest house at Machher for faculty and visitors, featuring private rooms, attached bathrooms, and quiet surroundings.

AMPHITHEATER

Amphitheater – A lovely outdoor venue on campus for events, performances, and lectures, set in a picturesque hillside environment.

TENNIS COURT

Tennis Court – Outdoor lawn tennis courts amidst the hills, used for practice and college tournaments, supporting an active and sporty campus life.

BARUSAHIB HELIPAD

Barusahib Helipad – A hilltop helipad on campus used for emergency airlifts, VIPs, and medical evacuations from nearby remote areas.

SOLAR PANEL

Solar Panels – A 220 kW, 978‑panel system generating ~600–700 kWh/day for campus needs—solar-powered cooking, lighting, heating—showcasing our green energy leadership.

TEACHERS RESIDENCE BUILDING

Teachers’ Residence Building – Multi-story, on-campus housing for faculty, offering furnished apartments and close proximity to university facilities.
